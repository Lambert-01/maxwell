WHO WE ARE

Meet the engineers behind MCL Consultants

Maxwell Consultancy Ltd, founded in 2003 and officially registered in Rwanda in 2008, has grown into one of East Africa's premier consulting firms. With 62 professionals, we specialize in design, supervision, feasibility studies, and innovative engineering solutions.

MPAGAZEHE ROBERT

Managing Director

Eng. Mpagazehe Robert, an accomplished civil engineer and certified ACCA professional, leads Maxwell Consultancy Ltd with a commitment to excellence.
